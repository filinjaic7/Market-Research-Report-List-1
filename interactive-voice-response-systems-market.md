<p><p>Interactive Voice Response (IVR) systems are categorized into two main market types: on-premises and cloud-based. On-premises IVR systems are installed and managed within an organization's own infrastructure, providing greater control and customization but requiring significant upfront investment and maintenance. In contrast, cloud-based IVR systems are hosted on remote servers, offering scalability, lower initial costs, and easier integration with other applications. This model allows businesses to easily adapt their IVR capabilities to changing needs without the need for complex infrastructure management.</p></p>
