<p><p>The mobile gas detector market features several technologies catering to diverse applications. Semiconductor technology relies on changes in electrical conductivity caused by gas interaction, providing compact and cost-effective solutions. IR technology employs infrared light absorption to quantify gases, known for its accuracy and non-contact measurement.
