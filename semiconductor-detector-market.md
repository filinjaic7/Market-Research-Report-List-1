<p><p>The semiconductor detector market includes several key types, each with distinct characteristics. Surface barrier detectors utilize a p-n junction to detect particles and photons with high efficiency, suitable for applications in nuclear instrumentation. Lithium drifting detectors enhance energy resolution and are effective in gamma spectroscopy due to their ability to absorb energy efficiently. High purity germanium detectors offer superior energy resolution and are widely used in radiation detection, particularly for low-energy gamma rays, making them essential for precise measurement in various scientific fields.</p></p>
