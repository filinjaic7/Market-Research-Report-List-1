<p><ul><li>Resistive Sensors</li><li>Magnetic Sensors</li><li>Inductive Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pedal sensors market comprises three primary types: resistive sensors, magnetic sensors, and inductive sensors. Resistive sensors operate by measuring changes in resistance due to pedal movement, providing accurate feedback. Magnetic sensors utilize magnetic fields to detect pedal position, ensuring reliability and durability. Inductive sensors rely on electromagnetic fields to sense the proximity of metal objects, offering high precision. Each sensor type caters to specific applications in automotive and industrial settings, enhancing performance and safety across various systems.</p></p>
