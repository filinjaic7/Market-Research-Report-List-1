<p><p>The Raman-AFM market encompasses two key segments: Atomic Force Microscopy (AFM) and Raman Spectroscopy. AFM is a powerful imaging technique used for surface characterization at the nanoscale, providing topographical and mechanical information. In contrast, Raman Spectroscopy analyzes molecular vibrations to give insights into material composition and chemical structure. Together, these techniques enhance material analysis, offering complementary data that benefits industries such as materials science, nanotechnology, and biomedicine, thus driving growth in research and commercial applications.</p></p>
