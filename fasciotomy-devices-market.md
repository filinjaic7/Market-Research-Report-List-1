<p><p>The fasciotomy devices market encompasses various treatment modalities. Split-thickness skin grafts (STSG) are used to cover wounds and promote healing. Negative Pressure Wound Therapy (NPWT) aids in wound closure by applying suction, reducing swelling, and enhancing blood flow. Vessel loop techniques involve using loops to manage tissue and blood vessels during healing. Continuous External Tissue Expansion (CETE) stretches surrounding tissues to facilitate improved skin availability for repairs. The "Other" category includes emerging or less common methods to support wound healing and tissue restoration.</p></p>
