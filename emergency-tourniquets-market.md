<p><p>The Emergency Tourniquet Market is categorized into non-pneumatic and pneumatic types. Non-pneumatic tourniquets rely on manual tightening mechanisms, utilizing materials like fabric or plastic to compress the bleeding limb, offering quick application in emergencies. In contrast, pneumatic tourniquets use inflatable systems that apply pressure through air-filled bladders, providing more controlled and adjustable compression. Both types serve vital roles in controlling severe bleeding during trauma situations, with distinct applications depending on the severity of the injury and required level of pressure.</p></p>
