<p><ul><li>Dynamic</li><li>Static</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The powder mixers market is categorized into dynamic and static types. Dynamic mixers utilize motion, such as blades or agitators, to blend powders thoroughly, ensuring uniformity and efficient mixing. They are ideal for large-scale production. In contrast, static mixers rely on the natural flow of materials through a fixed pipeline, making them suitable for continuous processes. Both types serve various industries, including pharmaceuticals, food, and chemicals, catering to different mixing needs based on application and scale.</p></p>
