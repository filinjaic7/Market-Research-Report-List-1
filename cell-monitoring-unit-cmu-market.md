<p><p>A Cell Monitoring Unit (CMU) is a crucial component in battery management systems, ensuring optimal performance and safety of battery cells. The market can be divided into two primary types: centralized and modular. </p><p>In centralized systems, a single unit monitors multiple cells, providing streamlined management and reduced complexity.
