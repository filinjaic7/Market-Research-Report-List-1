<p><ul><li>Area Scan Camera</li><li>Line Scan Camera</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The CMOS industrial cameras market is divided into two main types: area scan cameras and line scan cameras. Area scan cameras capture a complete image at once, making them ideal for stationary applications and high-resolution imaging tasks. In contrast, line scan cameras capture images one line at a time, which is beneficial for continuous processes and high-speed applications, such as conveyor systems. Both types serve diverse sectors, including manufacturing, automation, and quality inspection, enhancing operational efficiency and accuracy.</p></p>
