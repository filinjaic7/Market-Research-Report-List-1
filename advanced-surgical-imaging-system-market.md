<p><strong>The Advanced Surgical Imaging System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Image intensifier C-arms</li><li>Flat Panel Detector C-arms (FPD C-arms)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Advanced Surgical Imaging System market is primarily divided into two types: Image Intensifier C-arms and Flat Panel Detector C-arms (FPD C-arms). Image Intensifier C-arms utilize traditional image intensifier technology to enhance images for real-time visualization during surgical procedures. In contrast, FPD C-arms employ flat panel detector technology, offering higher image quality, reduced radiation exposure, and improved portability. Both types are vital in surgical environments, enhancing precision in minimally invasive procedures and broadening the scope of diagnostic capabilities.</p></p>
