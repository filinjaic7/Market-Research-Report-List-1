<p><p>Continuous level measurement technologies are essential for accurately monitoring liquid and solid levels in various industries. Differential pressure measures the pressure difference between two points, indicating level changes. Guided wave radar uses a time-of-flight method along a probe for precise readings. Non-contact radar employs microwave signals to measure levels without physical interaction. Ultrasonic sensors use sound waves to gauge distance to the surface. Capacitance sensors detect changes in capacitance as levels fluctuate. Other methods may include laser and optical techniques.</p></p>
