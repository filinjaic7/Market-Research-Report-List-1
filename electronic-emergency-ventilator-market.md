<p><p>The electronic emergency ventilator market is categorized into two main types: non-invasive ventilation and multi-mode ventilation. Non-invasive ventilation refers to methods that support breathing without requiring intubation, utilizing masks or nasal interfaces to deliver air, making it suitable for patients with respiratory distress. Multi-mode ventilation combines various ventilation techniques, such as invasive and non-invasive methods, allowing for flexible treatment options tailored to diverse patient needs. Both types play a critical role in emergency care and critical respiratory support.</p></p>
