<p><p>The medical ultrasound devices market comprises two primary types: diagnostic ultrasound systems and therapeutic ultrasound systems. Diagnostic ultrasound systems are used for imaging and evaluating internal structures, aiding in the identification of various medical conditions through techniques like 2D, 3D, and Doppler ultrasound. In contrast, therapeutic ultrasound systems focus on treatment applications, such as pain relief and tissue repair, often utilized in physiotherapy and surgical procedures. Both types play crucial roles in modern healthcare, enhancing patient diagnosis and treatment.</p></p>
