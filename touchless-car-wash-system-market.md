<p><ul><li>Touchless In-bay Car Wash Systems</li><li>Touchless Conveyor Tunnel Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The touchless car wash system market is primarily divided into two types: touchless in-bay car wash systems and touchless conveyor tunnel systems. Touchless in-bay systems are stand-alone units where vehicles remain stationary while high-pressure water jets and detergents clean the car without physical brushes. In contrast, touchless conveyor tunnel systems involve a moving belt that transports vehicles through a series of cleaning stages, utilizing advanced technology to deliver a thorough wash without any physical contact to minimize surface damage.</p></p>
