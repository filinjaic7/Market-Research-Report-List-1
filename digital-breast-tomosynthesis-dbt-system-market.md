<p><p>The Digital Breast Tomosynthesis (DBT) market is primarily segmented into two types: 2D Digital Mammography Technology and 3D Digital Mammography Technology. 2D Digital Mammography uses flat images to detect abnormalities but may overlap issues, leading to less clarity. In contrast, 3D Digital Mammography Technology, or DBT, captures multiple layered images of breast tissue, enhancing detection accuracy and reducing false positives. Both technologies play critical roles in breast cancer screening and diagnosis, with 3D imaging offering improved diagnostic capabilities.</p></p>
