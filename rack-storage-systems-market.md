<p><p>Rack Storage Systems can be categorized into two main types: Static Installations and Dynamic Systems. Static Installations involve fixed shelving units that provide stable storage for items but require manual handling for access. In contrast, Dynamic Systems utilize automated mechanisms, such as conveyor belts and shuttles, to facilitate high-density storage and retrieval, optimizing space and efficiency. While Static Installations are simpler and cost-effective, Dynamic Systems enhance productivity by streamlining operations and enabling quicker access to stored goods.</p></p>
