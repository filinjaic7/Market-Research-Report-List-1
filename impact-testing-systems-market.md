<p><p>Impact testing systems encompass techniques like pendulum testing and drop weight testing, both essential for assessing material toughness and durability. Pendulum testing utilizes a swinging mass to deliver a precise impact force, measuring energy absorbed during fracture. In contrast, drop weight testing involves dropping a known weight from a specific height onto the test specimen, evaluating its resistance to sudden loads. These methods are crucial across industries such as automotive and aerospace, ensuring materials meet safety and performance standards under dynamic conditions.</p></p>
