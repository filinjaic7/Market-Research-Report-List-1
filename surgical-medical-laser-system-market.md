<p><p>The surgical medical laser system market comprises various types, including solid-state laser systems, gas laser systems, dye laser systems, and diode laser systems. Solid-state lasers utilize a solid gain medium for efficient energy conversion, ideal for precise surgeries. Gas lasers, using gas as the active medium, are effective for cutting and cauterizing tissues. Dye lasers, employing organic dyes, offer adjustable wavelengths suitable for specialized treatments. Diode lasers, compact and versatile, are widely used for soft tissue procedures due to their ease of integration and effectiveness.</p></p>
