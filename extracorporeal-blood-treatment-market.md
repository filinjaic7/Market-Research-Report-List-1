<p><p>The extracorporeal blood treatment market encompasses various therapies aimed at managing blood-related conditions. Hemodialysis involves filtering waste from the blood in patients with kidney failure through a machine. Acute dialysis is a temporary solution for patients experiencing sudden kidney dysfunction, providing urgent filtration. Apheresis is a process that selectively removes specific components, such as plasma or platelets, from the blood, often used in autoimmune disorders and certain blood disorders to treat patients by modifying their blood composition.</p></p>
