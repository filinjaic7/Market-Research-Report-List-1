<p><p>The coffee capsule piercer market comprises two primary types: ceramic piercing needles and metal piercing needles. Ceramic piercing needles are known for their durability and resistance to corrosion, providing a clean and precise perforation in coffee capsules. They offer the advantage of maintaining flavor integrity without imparting metallic notes. In contrast, metal piercing needles are typically made from stainless steel, offering strength and cost-effectiveness. They are widely preferred for their efficiency and reliable performance in extracting coffee from capsules, catering to diverse consumer needs.</p></p>
