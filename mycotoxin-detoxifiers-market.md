<p><p>The mycotoxin detoxifiers market encompasses two primary types: mycotoxin binders and mycotoxin modifiers. Mycotoxin binders are substances that physically attach to mycotoxins in feed or food, preventing their absorption in the gastrointestinal tract, thus reducing toxicity. Mycotoxin modifiers, on the other hand, chemically alter the structure of mycotoxins, transforming them into less harmful compounds. Both types are crucial for ensuring food safety and animal health by mitigating the effects of harmful mycotoxins in various agricultural products.</p></p>
