<p><p>The Bluetooth Audio IC market comprises two main types: Ordinary Bluetooth Audio Chips and Audio System-on-Chip (SoC) solutions. Ordinary Bluetooth Audio Chips are designed specifically for basic audio transmission, offering essential functionalities with lower complexity and cost. In contrast, Audio SoC integrates advanced audio processing, Bluetooth connectivity, and additional features like noise cancellation and voice assistance into a single chip. This allows for improved audio quality, reduced power consumption, and a compact design, catering to the growing demand for high-performance audio devices.</p></p>
