<p><ul><li>Rotary Screw</li><li>Reciprocating</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The compressor rental market is primarily divided into two types: rotary screw and reciprocating compressors. Rotary screw compressors are known for their continuous operation and efficiency, making them suitable for large-scale applications and constant air supply. In contrast, reciprocating compressors offer high pressure and are typically used for intermittent applications, providing versatility in various fields. Both types cater to diverse industries, including construction, manufacturing, and oil and gas, offering tailored solutions based on specific performance needs.</p></p>
