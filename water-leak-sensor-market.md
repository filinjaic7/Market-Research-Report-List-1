<p><strong>The Water Leak Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Contact Type</li><li>Non-contacting Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The water leak sensor market is divided into two main types: contact type and non-contacting type. Contact type sensors detect water presence through direct contact with the liquid, often utilizing conductive materials that trigger alerts. In contrast, non-contacting type sensors use methods like ultrasonic waves or moisture-sensing technologies to identify leaks from a distance, without direct interaction. Both types serve essential roles in various applications, including residential, commercial, and industrial settings, helping to prevent water damage and reduce maintenance costs.</p></p>
