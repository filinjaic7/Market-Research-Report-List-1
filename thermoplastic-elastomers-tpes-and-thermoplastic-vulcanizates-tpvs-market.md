<p><p>Thermoplastic elastomers (TPEs) are a class of polymers that combine the properties of rubber with the ease of processing typical of plastics. They offer flexibility, durability, and a wide temperature range, making them suitable for various applications, including automotive, consumer goods, and medical devices. Thermoplastic vulcanizates (TPVs) are a subset of TPEs that have been partially vulcanized, providing enhanced temperature resistance and mechanical properties. TPVs are particularly valued in demanding applications for their improved performance characteristics.</p><p>The Thermoplastic Elastomers (TPEs) and Thermoplastic Vulcanizates (TPVs) Market is anticipated to witness robust growth, projected at a CAGR of 5.6% during the forecast period. Additionally, rising consumer awareness of sustainable materials is pushing manufacturers to innovate in their TPE and TPV offerings.
