<p><p>The MLCC (Multi-Layer Ceramic Capacitor) array market is segmented into various types based on dielectric properties. C0G capacitors are temperature stable and offer low loss, ideal for high-frequency applications. X5R capacitors have moderate temperature stability and can handle larger capacitance values, making them suitable for decoupling and filtering. X7R capacitors offer a wider capacitance range but with less stability than X5R, making them versatile for diverse applications. Other types include specialized formulations catering to unique operational requirements.</p></p>
