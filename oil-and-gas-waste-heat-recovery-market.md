<p><p>The Oil and Gas Waste Heat Recovery market comprises various technologies designed to capture and reuse waste heat generated during oil and gas operations. Heat exchangers transfer thermal energy between fluids, enhancing efficiency. Boilers utilize waste heat to produce steam for energy. Heat Recovery Steam Generators (HRSG) convert heat from gas turbines into steam. Turbines leverage recovered heat to generate additional electricity. Tanks store recovered heat, enabling its use in various processes, maximizing energy efficiency and reducing emissions across the sector.</p></p>
