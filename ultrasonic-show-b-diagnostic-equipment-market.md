<p><p>The Ultrasonic Show-B Diagnostic Equipment market is primarily categorized into two types: Continuous Wave Ultrasound Equipment and Pulsed Wave Ultrasound Equipment. Continuous Wave Ultrasound Equipment provides uninterrupted sound waves, ideal for measuring flow rates and velocities in various applications. In contrast, Pulsed Wave Ultrasound Equipment emits short bursts of sound waves, allowing for detailed imaging and precise measurement of distances, making it suitable for complex diagnostics and assessments. Both types play crucial roles in medical diagnostics and industrial applications.</p></p>
