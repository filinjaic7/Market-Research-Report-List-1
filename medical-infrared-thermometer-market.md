<p><strong>The Medical Infrared Thermometer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Forehead Type Thermometer</li><li>Ear Type Thermometer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical infrared thermometer market comprises primarily two types: forehead type and ear type thermometers. Forehead thermometers, or temporal thermometers, utilize infrared technology to measure body temperature from the forehead, providing quick, non-invasive readings. Ear thermometers, or tympanic thermometers, measure temperature by scanning the infrared heat generated by the eardrum, offering accuracy and speed. Both types are widely used in clinical settings and households for their convenience and ability to monitor fever efficiently, especially in pediatric care.</p></p>
