<p><strong>The Dental Resin Cements Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Light-cure</li><li>Dual-cure</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dental resin cements market comprises three main types: light-cure, dual-cure, and others. Light-cure cements require exposure to a specific wavelength of light for polymerization, offering precision and control during application. Dual-cure cements combine both light and chemical curing, making them versatile for varying clinical situations. Other types encompass self-cure and universal cements, which cater to specific needs and preferences. Each type is engineered to provide strong bonding, aesthetic appeal, and durability in dental restorations.</p></p>
