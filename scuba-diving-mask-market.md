<p><p>Scuba diving masks are categorized into three main types based on their design. One-window masks have a single, wide lens, offering an unobstructed view and enhancing peripheral vision. Two-window masks consist of two separate lenses, allowing for a customizable fit and better comfort for some divers. Panoramic masks feature a broad, continuous lens design, providing an expansive field of vision and minimizing blind spots. Each type caters to different preferences and diving experiences, enhancing visibility underwater.</p></p>
